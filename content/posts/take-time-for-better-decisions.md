Better decisions are directly proportional to the time spent on taking it. If enough time is allocated, we can examine the situation, evaluate the trade-offs and come up with the best possibilities to produce more favourable results.

Less time frame may produce creative results but stable outcomes need careful thought process. And it needs time.
