title: "How do you know if you are interested in learning something?"
excerpt: "Signs you are interested in learning."
updatedOn: "2020-04-03"
imageUrl: ""
tags: ["thought"]

If you are truly interested in learning something, you will give it a deep thought, you will come up with a lot of questions and you will take effort to search for the answers and you will never wait for someone to answer the question. I always remember Seth Godin's words, "No one has ever learnt anything against their will. Sooner or later it becomes voluntary."
