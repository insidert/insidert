A friend of mine asked if I could help him mix two audio files. I suggested audacity software. But that did not install correctly on his machine. I looked for alternatives and found a smaller software than audacity. We connected over anydesk and I walked him through the installation and mixing steps.

Later that day he called me and told he was very happy with the result and thanked me so much for the help.

I recommend thanking people if they received any help. It makes givers give more.
