A friend of mine asked if I could help him mix two audio files. I suggested audacity software. That did not install correctly on his machine. I spent some time looking for alternatives and later we connected over anydesk and I walked him through the installation and mixing steps.

Later that day he called me and told that he was very happy with the result and thanked me so much for the help.

I recommend thanking people if you receive any help; no matter how small it is.

People who give will give more.
