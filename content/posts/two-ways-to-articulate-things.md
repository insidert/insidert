I things we need to understand things. We all do. But how do we do that? How can you articulate things better? Well, I think there are two ways.

## 1. Read more

You need to read more about a subject to understand it deeply. Only then we can articulate why things are the way they are.

## 2. Deep thinking

Deep thinking is like going through all the probabilities and possibilities about something and coming up with a better solution and why it is a better solution.
