I like to play with different technologies to build my blog. I have already written a [post about it](/posts/thoughts-on-my-new-blog). Again I redesigned my blog. Previously it was on Heroku with Express JS and MongoDB. The only concern I have is the start time. I am using the Heroku free dyno and it takes 3-4 seconds of initial load which we do not want to have in this fast-moving times. I am already maintaining Coder Bootcamp blog with Gridsome and thought why can't I migrate my blog too. So I exported the posts from MongoDB and turned them into Markdown posts.

You can find the project repository on [GitHub.](https://github.com/insidert/insidert)
